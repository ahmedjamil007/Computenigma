Registration Procedure

Payment Submission:

Interested participants will have to directly pay in advance for their participation fee by using Mobile Banking Payment Method "bKash".

Payment Procedure for paying through bKash:

  1. Go to bKashMenu by dialing *247#
  2. Choose 'Send Money' option by pressing '1'
  3. Enter our billfold number: 01970713131.
  4. Enter BDT.####BDT
  5. Enter a reference against your payment: IUPC/Bot Raceing/LFR........
  6. Now enter your PIN to confirm: XXXX
  7. Please wait for confirmation text from bKash.
  8. Please save the confirmation text after receiving it
  9. Write down the payment code to the registration form.
  10. Done!
